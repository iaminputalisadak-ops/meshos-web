# Affiliate (Promoter) System – Business Rules

- **Promoter approval:** Admin must approve; no auto-approval.
- **Commission:** 10% on order final amount, for all products.
- **When commission is given:** Only after order status = **delivered** (not on order placed).
- **Order cancelled/refunded:** No commission; if already approved, reverse it.
- **Referral validity:** 30 days (cookie/session). Last click wins.
- **Attribution:** Last click wins (last promoter link clicked gets credit).
- **Self-referral:** Not allowed (promoter cannot earn on own order; match email/phone).
- **Withdrawal:** Manual payout by admin. Minimum withdrawal: ₹500.
